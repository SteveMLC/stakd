## 2. Play Store — Full description (max 4,000 chars, plain text)

Sort the crates. Build the empire.

Warehouse Sort is a satisfying sort puzzle wrapped in a warehouse-tycoon you can watch grow. Every shipment you sort pays cash to your wallet, fills your XP bar, and pushes your warehouse one step closer to the next big unlock. It is the calm, tappable puzzle you already love, with a reason to come back tomorrow.

THE GAME LOOP

Tap a bay to pick up the top crate. Tap another bay to drop it. Match colors, fill a bay end to end, and ship it out. Sort every bay to clear the level. Every clear earns cash and XP. Cash buys upgrades. XP levels up the warehouse. New levels open new content. Simple, then deep.

WHAT YOU GET IN v1.0

- Two warehouse tiers: start in your Local Warehouse, save up, and buy the Regional Hub for a permanent 1.5x earnings boost.
- Warehouse Levels 1 through 25 with milestone unlocks along the way.
- Frozen crates appear at Warehouse Level 5. Tap twice to thaw, then sort like normal. They pay more, too.
- 30 hand-tuned levels across Local and Regional, plus endless procedural generation past Level 31.
- Four power-ups for tricky boards: Dynamite Crate, Re-Route Shipment, Bay Crane, and Foreman's Advice.
- Forklift cosmetic shop. Three skins to earn: Red Sport, Blue Heavy, and Gold Premium. Your warehouse, your style.
- Daily Contract. One chance per day, three levels, triple cash payout. Same seed for every player.
- 7-day streak with a rotating exclusive cosmetic on Day 7.
- Star ratings (one, two, or three) on every level. Beat par, skip the undo, hit three stars.

WHY THIS ONE FEELS DIFFERENT

Most sort puzzles end the moment you clear the board. Warehouse Sort keeps paying. Cash flies to your wallet on every shipment. Your XP bar fills. Hit a milestone and the whole screen celebrates a new unlock. Come back tomorrow and your warehouse looks a little bigger, a little richer. Progress in place, constantly.

CASUAL FRIENDLY, NO PRESSURE

- No time limits in standard play. Think as long as you want.
- One free undo every level, plus more on demand.
- Stuck? The game spots dock jams and offers a way out: watch an ad for a new bay, undo, restart, or skip.
- Play offline. Auto-save. One-handed portrait mode.

SOUNDS GOOD, FEELS GOOD

Crisp crate slides, satisfying bay-complete chimes, gentle haptics on every tap, and a warehouse soundtrack designed to keep you in the flow. Sound, music, and haptics all toggle in Settings.

FAIR MONETIZATION

Free to play. Optional Remove Ads for 2.99 USD removes interstitials forever. Rewarded ads are always optional and always earn you something useful (extra bay, double cash, bonus power-up). No pay-to-win, no energy timers, no lives system.

WHO THIS IS FOR

- Fans of color sort, ball sort, and water sort puzzles who want more depth.
- Casual players who like a clear sense of growth between sessions.
- Anyone who loves the satisfying click of a fully sorted bay.

ABOUT GO7STUDIO

Made by a small team that ships games we want to play. Audio licensed from Mixkit and Uppbeat.
